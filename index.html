Research

Here you can find my research projects (published and to-be-published papers alike).

On The Use of Machine Learning for Home User Network Classification
  • Developed in late 2022 for the ML5G research challenge by the International Telecommunications Union (United Nations)
  • The goal of the work was to develop a machine learning model that can use indicators from DPI probe readings to determine if a broadbank network user is having a good or bad internet experience
  • Winner of the Best Student Solution and 2nd in category
  • Work soon to be published in the journal ITU J-FET

Application of Language Models Towards the Improvement of NVD entries and CVSS metrics

  • Started work in late 2022 in collaboration with Professor Sze-Yiu Chau at the Chinese University of Hong Kong
  • The goal of the work is to develop an NLP model that can use threat descriptions to fill in gaps in the National Vulnerability Database (NVD) and provide more accurate Common Vulnerability Scoring System (CVSS) severity metrics for documented threats
  • Project is currently in progress, with the expectation of a first draft by Summer 2023
